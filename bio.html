Bio

I was born and raised in Mountain View, California. I spent five years at University of Puget Sound in the pacific northwest getting pursuing music education. After getting a Masters of Arts in Teaching, I found that although I loved both music and education, the public school system was not for me. I spent the next school year as an academic consultant for Goal Oriented Academics, before pursuing something I found both interesting and enjoyable: programming. I am now on my way to becoming a junior developer as I continue my path of learning with Dev Bootcamp.


I am a man of many hobbies and interests. I play saxophone and piano, and my favorite genres are rap, jazz, and classical. I am fascinated by musical analysis of rap music, and I am currently working on jazz piano voicings. On downtime I enjoy climbing, chess, and meditation.


Fun Fact: I knew the names of many dinosaurs before I knew all of my colors.
